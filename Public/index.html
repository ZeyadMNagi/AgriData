About Our Project

Our primary goal is to support farmers in safeguarding their agricultural fields and crops from potential harm.
To achieve this, we have developed an innovative application that harnesses NASA's datasets to anticipate and notify farmers about upcoming floods, heat waves, and other potential disasters that could have an adverse impact on agriculture.





About Our Challenge

We are a team working on the challenge of leveraging Earth Observation Data for informed agricultural decision-making.
Our diverse skill set includes expertise in climate science, coding, data analysis, hardware, public speaking, software development, and web development.
We communicate in both Arabic and English, ensuring effective collaboration and outreach.
